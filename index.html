Our Legacy

Where Luxury Meets Life

KST Central Park is not simply a residence — it is a philosophy of living. Conceived with an obsessive attention to detail, every element has been thoughtfully designed to elevate your everyday existence.

Nestled alongside the Pune–Bangalore National Highway (NH-4), our address places you at the heart of connectivity while sheltering you in serene, curated surroundings. D-Mart proximity ensures urban convenience without compromising on peace.

KST is a proud member of NAREDCO — the National Real Estate Development Council — reflecting our unwavering commitment to transparency, quality, and excellence.
